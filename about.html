Our Background

Who we are & What we do?

Culinary Chronicles stands as a vibrant testament to the rich cultural tapestry of Aklan, Philippines. Founded in 2023 by the esteemed Akeanon native, Villanueva, this culinary venture serves as an immersive platform dedicated to unveiling the arts and heart of Akeanon culture through the lens of its authentic cuisine.

Specializing in Aklan's native culinary treasures, Culinary Chronicles meticulously curates and presents a diverse array of dishes that encapsulate the region's unique flavors and traditions. From traditional delicacies to contemporary interpretations, every offering tells a story, celebrating the legacy and culinary heritage of Aklan.

With a commitment to excellence, Culinary Chronicles not only tantalizes taste buds but also serves as a cultural ambassador, fostering a deeper understanding and appreciation for Akeanon traditions. Through events, collaborations, and culinary experiences, Culinary Chronicles opens a gateway to the soulful and flavorful world of Aklan, inviting both locals and global enthusiasts to savor the essence of this culturally rich and gastronomically vibrant region.
